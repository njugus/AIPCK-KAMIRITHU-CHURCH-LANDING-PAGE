About Us

Who Are We?

We are the AIPCK Kamirithu Church Youth, a vibrant group from AIPCK Kamirithu Church located in Kamirithu, Limuru, Kiambu County. As Christ's Ambassadors, we dedicate ourselves to serving the Almighty God through various activities. Our fellowship includes worship, singing, dancing, and engaging in fun events like Youth Fun Day. We also actively participate in fundraising efforts to support projects that foster the growth and advancement of our church community. Our commitment to serve Him is unwavering, and we meet every Sunday after service to share in this mission. Everyone is welcome to join us! Join us and be part of a community where faith and fellowship flourish together!
